WHERE THE MONEY GOES
100% of donations collected on this website will go to building Life Teaches core outreach activities, including, but not limited to, live speaking engagements, supportive online presence, as well as providing coaching and support services. Donations received will help offset the cost of resources needed to reach out to indivuals across the country, as well as develop an online platform where everyone can engage with valuable content, access supportive resources and utilize crisis support services too.
